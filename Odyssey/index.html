Coastal Bliss & Ocean Breezes

Beach

These beaches showcase the natural beauty and allure of Vietnam and Chile, attracting visitors with their picturesque settings and opportunities for relaxation and water activities.

Ha Long Bay Beach

Located in Quang Ninh Province, Ha Long Bay is known for its stunning limestone karsts rising from emerald waters. The beach offers scenic views, boat trips, and opportunities for kayaking and swimming.

Phu Quoc Island Beach

Phu Quoc Island, off the coast of Vietnam, is famous for its pristine beaches. Long Beach, in particular, is a popular spot with its white sandy shores, clear turquoise waters, and beachfront resorts.

Anakena Beach

Situated on Easter Island (Rapa Nui), Anakena Beach is a picturesque beach with golden sand and crystal-clear waters. Surrounded by palm trees and ancient moai statues, it offers a unique blend of natural beauty and cultural significance.

Gushing Waters and Pristine Beauty

waterfalls

These waterfalls showcase the natural wonders and scenic landscapes found in Vietnam and Chile, captivating visitors with their beauty and creating memorable experiences.

Iguazu Falls

While primarily located in Argentina, Iguazu Falls also extends into Chile, marking the border between the two countries. This awe-inspiring waterfall system comprises a series of cascades and is renowned for its sheer size and power.

Maqui Falls

Situated in the Huilo-Huilo Biological Reserve in southern Chile, Maqui Falls is a striking waterfall surrounded by lush rainforest. It offers a picturesque setting and provides a glimpse into Chile's pristine natural beauty.

Ban Gioc-Detian Falls

Located on the border between Vietnam and China, Ban Gioc-Detian Falls is one of the largest and most impressive waterfalls in Southeast Asia. It consists of multiple cascades and offers breathtaking views amidst lush greenery.

Alpine Wonders and Nature's Majesty

Mountains

These mountain landscapes in Vietnam and Chile offer awe-inspiring vistas, tranquil surroundings, and opportunities for outdoor adventures, making them popular destinations for nature lovers and photographers.

Torres del Paine

Located in the Patagonia region of Chile, Torres del Paine National Park is known for its stunning mountain landscapes. The park features granite peaks, glaciers, turquoise lakes, and vast open plains, creating a dramatic and visually captivating mountainous environment.

Aconcagua

Situated in the Andes mountain range in central Chile, Aconcagua is the highest peak in the Americas. Its majestic snow-capped summit and surrounding peaks provide a breathtaking mountain landscape that attracts mountaineers and nature enthusiasts from around the world.

Fansipan Mountain

Located in the Hoang Lien Son mountain range in northern Vietnam, Fansipan Mountain is the highest peak in the country. It offers breathtaking views of lush green valleys, terraced rice fields, and majestic mountain ranges, providing a stunning and picturesque landscape.

The Valdivian Rainforest

located in southern Chile, is one of the world's temperate rainforests. It is known for its lush vegetation, ancient trees, and diverse ecosystem. Visitors can explore the forest through hiking trails, witness cascading waterfalls, and encounter a variety of bird and animal species.

Cat Tien National Park

Located in southern Vietnam, a tropical forest known for its rich biodiversity. The park is home to diverse wildlife, including endangered species like the Indochinese tiger and Javan rhinoceros. Visitors can explore the lush forest through trekking trails, spot wildlife, and experience the beauty of nature.
